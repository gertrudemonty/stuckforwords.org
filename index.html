A few words about me.

As a freelance copywriter I enjoy collaborating with small businesses and accompanying them on the journey they take through the transformation of online presentation. As a Dublin based actor turned literature student turned copywriter I have a natural ability to visualise the emotional context of the product. I have learned when sharp and persuasive language is required for forward developing products. Equally as important are the calming and reflective tones necessary for the more established or sophisticated client. Working closely with colleagues from varied disciplines, I find I communicate well with many, and easily relate how the customer will view your advertising. I intend to keep the project on track. When taken from the page to the stage, the message never loses its intention.
